Summary

Inception is a science fiction movie about Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters people's dreams to steal secrets. He is given a task to plant an idea into someone's mind. The journey involves multiple dream layers, emotional conflicts, and challenges that blur the line between dream and reality.

Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio plays Dom Cobb. Other actors include Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, and Marion Cotillard.
